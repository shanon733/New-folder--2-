Welcome to Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. Founded in 1902, based in Madrid, Spain.

The club plays its home matches at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium and has millions of fans worldwide.

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1902
  • Country: Spain
  • Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu
  • League: La Liga
  • Nickname: Los Blancos
