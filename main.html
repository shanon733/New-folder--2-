About Real Madrid

History

Real Madrid was founded in 1902 and quickly became one of the strongest clubs in Spain. During the 1950s, the club dominated European football and won the first five European Cups.

Many legendary players have played for the club including Alfredo Di Stéfano, Zinedine Zidane, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Sergio Ramos.
